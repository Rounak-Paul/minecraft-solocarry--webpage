About

SoloCarrySMP is a small server hosted by The Cliffhouse Games featuring Paper for Minecraft Version 1.18.

What Is A SMP

SMP stands for Survival Multiplayer. An acronym for the Minecraft game mode that enables players to interact with each other as they try to survive. SMP is the default game mode when you create a server.

Our Vision For The SMP

Our server is originally inspired by the popular Hermitcraft series, which is a gathering of some popular content creators across the globe in a Minecraft SMP environment. We hope to build a likeminded community like Hermitcraft of our own.

Cliffhouse Games

Cliffhouse is a Gaming Community originally created by Kris. Our community hosts a variety of table top games as well as servers for popular multiplayer games. The servers are locally hosted and managed by our community head.
